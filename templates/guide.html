User Guide

  1. Click picture of garbage pile you want to be taken care of.
  2. Open Trashed.
  3. Enter image and its location in the form provided.
  4. Your submission will be sent to authorities if CNN algorithm classifies input image as trash.
  5. If CNN is unable to detect trash in image, then you can optionally click 'Report' to prompt authorities for error.
